💡 Tips for Players

🏆 Chromatic
Chromatic skins are the strongest and have the best abilities.
⚔️ Legendary
Legendary skins are very strong and have powerful attacks.
🌀 Mythic
Mythic skins have unique healing or invisibility abilities.
⚖️ Epic
Epic skins are balanced and have useful abilities.
🛡️ Rare
Rare skins are best for beginner players.
🎯 For Attack
Pick skins with high attack: Rainbow, Legend Bloom, Myth Pulse.
🛡️ For Defense
Pick skins with high defense: Rainbow, Legend Bloom, Aurora Titan.
💨 For Speed
Pick skins with high speed: Ninja, Shadow, Phantom.
❤️ For Stamina
Pick skins with high HP: Rainbow, Legend Bloom, Aurora Titan.

  • OS: Windows 11.
  • Processor: Intel Core i3 / AMD Ryzen 3 or equivalent

🔓 How to unlock skins?

  • Rare: Available from the start or for a small amount of coins.
  • Epic: Unlocked through achievements or shop purchases.
  • Mythic: Special events, missions, or loot boxes.
  • Legendary: Difficult challenges, high level, or limited-time events.
  • Chromatic: Rarest — available only during special events or as a 100% completion reward.

⚡ Special Abilities

Each skin in Battle Mode has a unique special ability that can change the course of battle. Abilities activate automatically under certain conditions or after pressing the appropriate button (depending on the game mode). Check the BattleMode tab for details.
